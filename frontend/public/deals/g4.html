Meet Murder Mystery Case File, the card game that combines strategy, memory, and a chilling zombie theme to keep you on your toes! With every flip of the card, you're one step closer to survival... or succumbing to the undead.

Key Features

Dual Gameplay

A thrilling strategy game that doubles as a party drinking game with optional Challenge Cards. Perfect for both intense game nights and lighthearted gatherings.

Fast-Paced Speed

Timing is key—beat your opponents to discard Phase and Power Cards, or risk being stuck with them.

Epic Showdown Ending

Rid your hand of all cards first to declare "NO DEAD AT MY DECK!" and leave your hand on the table. But watch out—if you're wrong, double penalties await!

Zombie Memory Showdown

Challenge your memory as you keep track of Phase and Power Cards, adding a level of skill that's unique among party games.
