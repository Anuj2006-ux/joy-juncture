Murder Mystery Game Night at Primarc Pecan HO, Mumbai

Corporate Events • Joy Juncture

100 Employees. 3 Murders. Endless Clues. Zero Boredom!

We conducted a large-scale Murder Mystery Game Night for nearly 100 employees, all playing simultaneously.

To make the experience seamless and interactive:

  • Everyone was divided into teams of 3
  • Each team received a custom-designed Murder Mystery Case File
  • The objective? Step into the shoes of detectives and crack The Bloody Inheritance, a thrilling case file created by Joy Juncture

What followed was 75 minutes of pure brainwork… mixed with laughter, collaboration, and a whole lot of competitive energy.

Why companies love this activity:

This wasn’t your usual “team-building activity.” Employees who had never participated in such an experience found themselves:

  • Debating motives

  • Discussing evidence
  • Working as detectives, not just colleagues

Teams bonded naturally... not through forced icebreakers, but through shared curiosity and the thrill of cracking a case together. It sparked collaboration, communication, logical thinking, and networking across departments, exactly what every corporate wants from an employee engagement session.
